Product Catalog

Overview:
The Product Catalog contains all windows, doors, and accessories you sell. Products have standard pricing that can be customized per quote.

How to Access:
- Click "Products" tab in navigation
- Or use App Launcher and search for "Products"

Finding Products:
1. Go to Products tab
2. Search by name or code
3. Or browse by category

Product Information:
Each product includes:
- Name: Product display name
- Product Code/SKU: Unique identifier
- Standard Price: Default price
- Category: Product grouping
- Active: Whether product is available

Step-by-Step Guide:

Adding Products to Quotes:
1. Open a quote
2. Click "Add Products"
3. Search or browse catalog
4. Select products
5. Enter quantity
6. Adjust price if needed

Understanding Price Books:
- Standard Price Book: Default pricing for all customers
- Custom Price Books: Special pricing for specific customers
- Volume Pricing: Discounts for large orders

Managing Products (Admins):
1. Go to Products tab
2. Click "New Product"
3. Enter details: Product Name, Product Code, Description, Category
4. Save
5. Add to Price Book with pricing

Common Issues:

"Product Not Found":
- Check spelling of product name/code
- Product may be inactive
- Contact admin to add new products

"Missing Price":
- Product needs a Price Book entry
- Go to Product, then Price Books, then Add Entry

Can't Edit Product:
- Only admins can modify products
- Request changes through your manager

Tips:
- Keep product names consistent
- Deactivate discontinued items (don't delete them)
- Review pricing quarterly
- Use product codes for faster searching
