# Cost and Price Overrides in bpmPro

This tutorial demonstrates how to apply cost and price overrides inside bpmPro, giving you full control over your quoting and pricing process. You'll learn how to adjust markup and cost dynamically, create manual overrides, reset items to auto-calculate, and apply bulk updates efficiently across multiple line items.

---

## What You'll Learn

- How to open and navigate the **Sales Document Workspace** and **Manage Items** page
- How to review preset items with their markup values
- How to exclude labor from a full-service quote using the **Exclude Labor** checkbox
- How to adjust markup and cost values and watch bpmPro automatically recalculate prices
- How to create a **manual price override** using the wrench tool
- What the **lock icon** means and how it indicates manually overridden pricing
- How to override the **product cost** and observe automatic margin adjustments
- How to **reset a line item** back to auto-calculation mode with one click
- How to perform a **mass markup update** across all line items
- How locked items are **protected and excluded** from mass updates

---

## Key Concepts

### Automatic Pricing

By default, bpmPro calculates prices automatically based on your markup percentage and product cost. When you change the markup, the price updates. When you change the cost, the margin adjusts. This keeps your numbers consistent without manual math.
