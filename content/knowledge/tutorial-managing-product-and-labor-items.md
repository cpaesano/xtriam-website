Managing Product and Labor Items

# Managing Product and Labor Items in bpmPro

The **Manage Items** functionality is where you build your sales document line by line. This tutorial walks through the complete process — from creating a new sales document to adding product and labor items, cloning items, performing mass updates, and sorting your proposal.

---

## Creating a Sales Document

1. Open an existing project
2. Click the **arrow** and select **New** to create a new sales document
3. Name the document (e.g., the project name + "Residence") — the name is customizable
4. Leave the default settings or adjust as needed
5. Click **Save**

The new sales document is created. Click into it to open the **Sales Document Workspace**, then navigate to the **Sales Document Navigator** and click **Manage Items**.

---

## Adding a New Line Item

Click the **Add New Item** button. Each line item has two sections:

### Product Details (Left Side)

Fill out the product information:

- **Quantity** — Number of units
- **Mark** — Location identifier (e.g., "Living Room", "Master Bedroom")
- **Width** and **Height** — Product dimensions
- **Vendor Quote Number** — Reference number from the vendor
- **Product Series** — The product line
- **Frame Finish** — Color/finish selection
- **Vendor Name** — Select the product vendor
- **Glass Makeup** — Glass specification
