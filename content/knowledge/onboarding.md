bpmPro Onboarding & Company Setup

Overview:
This guide covers the complete onboarding process for new bpmPro customers, including company setup, configuration, and training.

Welcome to bpmPro:
bpmPro is a software solution tailored for window and door contractors. It streamlines business processes, boosts productivity, and enhances customer service. As your technology partner, xTriam provides support from setup through ongoing training.

---

Getting Started - Company Settings:

Your journey with bpmPro starts with setting up your company information. This foundational step is crucial for tailoring the software to your specific needs.

Company Information:
- Enter your company's name
- Main phone number
- State license number
- Company abbreviation
This information appears on all proposals, contracts, change orders, and service tickets.

Digital Identity:
- Company address
- Company logo
- Email address
- Website URL
- Social media profiles

Financial Settings:
- Sales commission structures
- Convenience fees for credit card transactions

---

Sales Commission Setup:

Set up compensation models for your sales team to ensure they are motivated and aligned with your business goals.

---

Sales Documents Configuration:

Customize your terms and conditions with easy-to-use tools. You can adjust them according to your evolving needs.

---

Dealer Items (Price Book):

Define your price book for product installations and general labor, including costs for:
- Stucco repairs
- Drywall repairs
- Wood bucking
- Other installation-related tasks

---

Building Materials:

Configure the materials used in installations:
- Screws
- Wood
- Flashing
- Caulking
This ensures accurate costing and billing.

---

Add-On Products:

Expand your product offerings by configuring add-on products:
- Handles
- Levers
- Deadbolts
- Other accessories

---

Account Setup:

Maintain flexible tracking of:
- Vendors
- Municipalities
- Billing information
- Permits
- Employees
- Clients
- Professional services
- Installation contractors
- Main company email address

---

Calendar Setup:

bpmPro provides comprehensive calendar functionality for scheduling. Configure work assignments for each user license, covering:
- Product installations
- Building inspections
- Measuring appointments
- Final measurements

The calendar helps manage your team's workload effectively, ensuring every task is completed on time.

---

Stripe Account Setup:

Run through the Stripe setup wizard to provide all company information. This allows you to collect payments through the Stripe platform.

---

Training Sessions:

xTriam offers:
- Two on-premises training sessions (2 hours each)
- Continuous and unlimited ongoing training via Zoom or phone

The goal is to support your team's mastery of bpmPro so you can fully utilize its capabilities.

---

Onboarding Checklist:

Company Settings:
- Company Information entered
- Digital Identity configured
- Financial Settings set up

Sales Setup:
- Sales Commission models defined
- Sales Documents terms customized
- Dealer Items price book created

Product Setup:
- Building Materials configured
- Add-On Products added

Account Setup:
- Vendors, municipalities, and other accounts created
- Main company email established

Scheduling:
- Calendar configured for all work assignment types

Payments:
- Stripe account connected

Training:
- Initial training sessions completed
- Ongoing training scheduled as needed
