Sales Hub Dashboard

Overview:
The Sales Hub is a personalized home page designed for sales reps and estimators. It puts everything a salesperson needs on a single screen: active quotes, pipeline summary, quick stats, and quick access to recent work.

How to Access:
The Sales Hub is available as a tab in the bpmPro app. It can also be set as the default landing page.

Main Sections:

My Active Sales Documents:
A full table of every open quote you are working on. Each row shows:
- Document name and account
- Current status
- Dollar amount
- How long since it was last touched
- Color-coded by age: green (less than 14 days), orange (14-30 days), red (over 30 days)
Click any row to open that Sales Document.

Quick Create:
Start a new Sales Document in one click. The wizard opens as a popup on the same page so you never lose your place. Recent account cards let you start even faster by pre-selecting the customer.

My Pipeline:
Summary tiles showing how many quotes sit at each status (Unquoted, Being Quoted, Quoted) with total dollar values. Gives you a quick read on where your pipeline stands.

Quick Stats:
Monthly performance numbers updated in real time:
- Sales Documents created this month
- Documents sold
- Win rate percentage
- Total pipeline dollar value

Recently Worked:
The last 10 Sales Documents and Projects you viewed or modified. Shows status and dollar amount for quotes, or address and phone for projects.

Needs Attention:
The system flags stale quotes that have not moved forward:
- Quoted documents sitting more than 14 days
- Being Quoted for more than 30 days
- Unquoted for more than 7 days

E-Sign Activity:
Shows recent e-signature activity including documents sent for signature, viewed, and signed.

Common Issues:

Dashboard shows no data:
- Make sure you are logged in as a user with a mapped Contact record
- Your Contact must be linked via the Associated User field
- Check that you have Sales Documents assigned to you

Tips:
- Check the Needs Attention section daily to follow up on stale quotes
- Use Quick Create to start quotes faster instead of navigating through accounts
- The color-coded age indicators help prioritize which quotes to work on first
