Email Wizard & Notifications

Overview:
bpmPro includes email templates and automated notifications to keep customers and team members informed throughout the sales and installation process.

How to Access:
- From any Account, Contact, or Project record
- Click "Send Email" button
- Or use the Activity tab

Step-by-Step Guide:

Sending an Email:

Step 1: Open a Record
- Go to Account, Contact, or Project
- Click "Send Email" button

Step 2: Select Template
- Choose from pre-built templates
- Or compose custom email

Step 3: Personalize
- Merge fields auto-fill customer info
- Add custom message if needed
- Attach documents (quotes, receipts, etc.)

Step 4: Send
- Preview before sending
- Click "Send"
- Email is logged in activity history

Available Email Templates:
- Quote Follow-up: After sending a quote
- Deposit Request: To collect initial payment
- Installation Scheduled: Confirm installation date
- Installation Complete: After job is finished
- Thank You / Review Request: Ask for reviews/referrals

Team Notifications:
Automatic alerts sent to team members:
- New lead assigned: Sales rep notified
- Quote approved: Sales rep and Manager notified
- Payment received: Project manager and Admin notified
- Project status change: Assigned team members notified

Managing Notifications:
1. Go to Setup, then Notifications
2. Enable/disable notification types
3. Set preferred delivery (email, in-app, or both)

Common Issues:

"Email Not Sent":
- Check recipient email address is valid
- Verify your email sending limits
- Check spam folder on receiving end

"Template Not Found":
- Template may be inactive
- Contact admin to activate templates

Not Receiving Notifications:
- Check notification settings
- Verify email isn't going to spam
- Check if notifications are enabled for your role

Tips:
- Personalize templates with your voice
- Follow up within 24 hours of sending quotes
- Use email tracking to see when emails are opened
- Keep templates updated with current information
