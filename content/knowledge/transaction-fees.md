Transaction Fees

Overview:
Transaction Fees allow you to add processing fees (such as credit card convenience fees) to project invoices. The fee is calculated automatically and appears as a separate line item on the invoice PDF.

How to Access:
Open the Invoice Payment Console for any Project Invoice. The Transaction Fee option is available within the console.

How to Add a Transaction Fee:
1. Open the Project Invoice
2. Go to the Invoice Payment Console
3. Click "Add Transaction Fee"
4. Enter the fee percentage (e.g., 3 for a 3% credit card fee) or a flat dollar amount
5. The fee is calculated based on the invoice amount
6. The fee appears as a clearly labeled line item on the invoice PDF

How It Works:
- The fee is calculated on the invoice subtotal
- It shows as a separate line so the customer can see exactly what the charge covers
- Transaction fees can be added, edited, and deleted with confirmation prompts
- The total invoice amount includes the transaction fee

Example:
Invoice subtotal: $10,000
Credit card fee (3%): $300
Invoice total: $10,300

Common Issues:

Fee amount seems wrong:
- Verify the percentage or flat amount entered
- The fee is calculated on the invoice subtotal, not the grand total

Tips:
- Use transaction fees when passing credit card processing costs to the customer
- The fee is clearly labeled on the PDF so the customer understands the charge
- You can remove the fee if the customer chooses a different payment method (check or ACH)
