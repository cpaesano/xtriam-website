Preset Products

Overview:
Preset Products are saved product packages that let you quickly add a standard set of items to a Sales Document. Instead of adding products one by one, you can select a preset and all its items are added at once.

How to Access:
On a Sales Document, look for the Preset Products option in the Manage Items (A3) section.

How to Use Presets:

Applying a Preset:
1. Open a Sales Document
2. Go to the Manage Items (A3) section
3. Select a preset from the list
4. The preset's products are added to the document with default dimensions and pricing
5. Adjust individual items as needed (dimensions, quantities, pricing)

Creating Presets:
1. Navigate to the Preset Products management area
2. Click "New Preset"
3. Use the dependent picklist filters to select products (Series, Type, Brand, Height, Width)
4. Build your preset by adding products
5. Save the preset with a descriptive name

Dependent Picklist Filtering:
When building a preset, the system uses cascading filters:
- Select a Series first
- Type options filter based on the selected series
- Brand filters based on type
- Height and width options follow

Tips:
- Create presets for your most common job types to save time on quoting
- After applying a preset, customize individual items for the specific project
- Presets are shared across all users in your organization
