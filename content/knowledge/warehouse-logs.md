Warehouse Logs

Overview:
Warehouse Logs track the receiving, storage, and movement of materials for product orders. Each log records activity type, piece counts, fulfillment status, and any issues.

How to Access:
From a Product Order record, click "Create Warehouse Log" or navigate to the Warehouse Logs related list.

Creating a Warehouse Log:
1. Open the Product Order
2. Click "Create Warehouse Log"
3. Select the activity type (Receiving, Shipping, Transfer, etc.)
4. Enter piece counts and status
5. Note any issues or damage
6. Save the log

What Gets Tracked:
- Activity type and date
- Number of pieces received vs expected
- Fulfillment status
- Issues or damage notes
- Who created the log and when

Common Issues:

Piece count mismatch:
- Compare the warehouse log piece count against the product order
- Document any shortages or damage in the notes field

Tips:
- Create a warehouse log immediately upon receiving materials
- Document any damage or missing pieces right away for vendor claims
- Use warehouse logs to confirm all materials are on-site before scheduling installation
