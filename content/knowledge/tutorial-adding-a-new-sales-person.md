# Adding a New Sales Person in bpmPro

To add a new sales person in bpmPro, you create them as a **contact** under your company's account and assign them the **Sales and Estimating** internal company role. This ensures they appear in sales person dropdowns throughout the system — such as when creating quotes, proposals, and projects.

> **Note:** This same process applies to adding any employee (project managers, operations staff, etc.) — the only difference is the Internal Company Role you select.

---

## Step 1 — Find Your Company Account

Use the **global Search box** at the top of bpmPro to search for your company name. Click on the **Account** result to open your company's account record.

---

## Step 2 — Go to the Contacts Tab

Inside the account record, click on the **Contacts** tab to see all contacts currently linked to the account.

---

## Step 3 — Click New

In the Contacts section, click the **New** button to create a new contact.

---

## Step 4 — Fill Out Contact Information

Fill out the new contact form with the sales person's details:

- **First Name** and **Last Name** (required)
- **Title** — Enter the employee's title (e.g., "Sales Rep")

---

## Step 5 — Set Category to Employee

Click the **Category** dropdown and select **Employee**. This is important — it identifies this contact as a team member rather than a customer.
