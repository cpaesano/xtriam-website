Credit Memos

Overview:
Credit Memos allow you to apply adjustments, discounts, or write-offs to project invoices. They reduce the balance due without recording an actual payment.

How to Access:
Open the Invoice Payment Console for any Project Invoice. The Credit Memo section is available within the console.

Types of Credit Memos:
- Write-Off: When you decide not to collect part of the balance
- Adjustment: Correcting an invoice amount
- Discount: Applying a negotiated discount after the invoice was issued

Creating a Credit Memo:
1. Open the Invoice Payment Console
2. Click "Add Credit Memo"
3. Select the type (Write-Off, Adjustment, Discount)
4. Enter the amount
5. Add a description explaining the reason
6. The credit is applied to the invoice balance immediately

Impact on Invoice:
- Credit memos reduce the balance due on the invoice
- They appear as separate line items in the payment history
- The invoice status updates automatically (e.g., from Partially Paid to Paid if the credit covers the remaining balance)

Common Issues:

Credit memo not reducing balance:
- Verify the credit memo is linked to the correct invoice
- Check that the amount is entered correctly

Need to reverse a credit memo:
- Delete the credit memo from the console
- The balance will be restored automatically

Tips:
- Always include a description for audit trail purposes
- Use credit memos instead of changing invoice amounts to maintain a clear paper trail
- Credit memos are different from chargebacks. Chargebacks are used for vendor-related adjustments on deal sheets.
