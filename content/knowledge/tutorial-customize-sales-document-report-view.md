# Customize Sales Document Report View in bpmPro

Before sending a proposal or contract to a customer, you can customize exactly what information appears in the report. The **Customize Report View** menu gives you toggle controls to show or hide sections, so you can present a clean, professional document every time.

---

## Opening the Customize Report View

If the report options aren't visible by default, click the **plus icon** in the top-right corner to expand the menu. You'll see a set of toggle buttons organized into three categories.

---

## Available Toggle Options

### Pricing and Quote Details

| Toggle | What It Controls |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Product and Labor Breakdown** | Shows or hides the detailed cost breakdown by product and labor |
| **Line Item Pricing** | Shows or hides individual line item prices |
| **Vendor Summary** | Shows or hides the vendor summary section |

### Content Display

| Toggle | What It Controls |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Cover Page** | Shows or hides the proposal cover page |
| **Product Illustrations** | Shows or hides product drawings for each line item |
| **Labor Scope of Work** | Shows or hides the labor scope details |

### Technical Specifications

| Toggle | What It Controls |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Product Dimensions** | Shows or hides width and height for each line item |
| **Design Pressure** | Shows or hides design pressure ratings |
| **Show Energy Values** | Shows or hides energy performance
