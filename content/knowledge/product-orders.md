Product Orders

Overview:
Product Orders track the purchasing and delivery of products from vendors for your projects. Each order tracks status, amounts, shipping dates, and warehouse receiving.

How to Access:
From the Project record page, navigate to the Product Orders related section. Or use the Command Center to access the Product Orders list.

Product Order Console:
The console shows all details for a product order:
- Order status and dates (order date, ship date, warehouse date, close date)
- Vendor information
- Amounts: sales order amount, invoice amount, balance due
- Shipping and accounting notes
- Reorder and warranty flags

Order Status Flow:
Track your order from placement through delivery:
- Ordered: Purchase order sent to vendor
- In Production: Vendor is manufacturing
- Shipped: Products are in transit
- Received: Products arrived at warehouse
- Closed: Order complete

Managing Orders:
1. Create a Product Order from the Project
2. Link it to the vendor/supplier
3. Enter order amounts and dates
4. Update status as the order progresses
5. Add notes for shipping or accounting

Common Issues:

Order not showing on project:
- Verify the Product Order is linked to the correct Project
- Check that the Sales Document reference is correct

Tips:
- Update order status promptly so the team knows where materials stand
- Use the warehouse date to plan installation scheduling
- Add shipping notes with tracking numbers for easy reference
