Installation Notes

> **Purpose:** Enter notes from the sales rep or project manager that the installation team needs to see on-site. These notes print directly on the Installation Ticket.

---

## Where to Enter Installation Notes

Open the Sales Document and click the **Summary** tab. Scroll down to the **Notes** section. You will find the **Installation Notes (Internal)** field at the bottom of that section.

Enter your instructions in that field. These notes are **internal** — they are not shown on the client-facing Sales Document.

---

## Where They Appear

Once saved, the Installation Notes print in the **Installation Notes** section at the bottom of the Installation Ticket, under a blue left border labeled **Installation Notes**.

> **Note:** The Installation Ticket also shows a separate **Sales Document Notes from Sales Person to Client** section (highlighted in orange). That is a different field. Make sure crew-specific instructions go into **Installation Notes (Internal)** on the Sales Document, not in the client-facing notes.

---

## Quick Reference

| Field | Location | Visible To |
|-------|----------|------------|
| Installation Notes (Internal) | Sales Document → Summary → Notes | Installation team only |
| Sales Document Notes | Sales Document → Summary → Notes | Client-facing |

---

## Who Should Use This

- **Sales reps** — to flag special site conditions or client preferences at the time of sale
- **Project managers** — to add or update instructions before the installation
