# Updating Product Installation Items in bpmPro

bpmPro stores all your dealer item pricing and material settings in the **Dealer Items** section of the Command Center. These settings control installation costs for new construction and retrofit jobs, additional work and equipment charges, screw selections, woodbuck specifications, caulking, and flashing — all organized into six list views that you can edit inline.

---

## Step 1 — Navigate to Dealer Items

Go to the **Command Center** and scroll down to the **Labor Pricebook Setup** section. Click **Dealer Items** to open the dealer items list.

---

## Step 2 — Select a List View

The Dealer Items page opens to your pinned (default) list view. Click the **dropdown arrow** next to the list view name to switch between views.

bpmPro provides six pre-built list views:

| List View | What It Controls |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **1 ~ Product Installation Items** | New construction and retrofit installation costs per product |
| **2 ~ Additional Work and Equipment** | Drywall, stucco, ladder, and scaffolding charges |
| **3 ~ Screws for Items** | Product and woodbuck screw selections and spacing |
| **4 ~ Woodbucks for Items** | Woodbuck material selection and pricing |
| **5 ~ Caulking for Items** | Caulking product selection per dealer item |
| **6 ~ Flashing for Items** | Flashing specifications per dealer item |
