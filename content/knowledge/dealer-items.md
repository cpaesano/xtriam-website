Dealer Items

Overview:
Dealer Items are the products and equipment in your catalog that can be added to Sales Documents. Each dealer item represents a specific product from a vendor with its pricing, dimensions, and specifications.

How to Access:
From the Command Center, click "Dealer Items" or navigate to the Dealer Items tab. On a Sales Document, use the Dealer Item Lookup when adding line items.

Managing Dealer Items:

Viewing Items:
1. Open the Dealer Items list
2. Filter by product type, series, or vendor
3. Click any item to see full details including images

Adding Items to a Sales Document:
1. On the Sales Document, go to the A3 (Manage Items) section
2. Use the Dealer Item Lookup to search
3. Select the item and configure dimensions (width, height)
4. Set quantities and pricing

Item Details:
Each dealer item includes:
- Product series and configuration
- Brand and vendor
- Dimensions and specifications
- Images (if uploaded)
- Default pricing

Tips:
- Use filters to quickly find products by type or series
- Dealer items with images help sales reps identify products faster
- Product pricing on dealer items serves as the default but can be overridden on individual line items
