Commission Payout Manager

Overview:
The Commission Payout Manager automatically calculates and tracks sales commissions based on your company's commission structure. Sales reps can view their earnings, and managers can process payouts.

How to Access:
- Click "My Commissions" tab for personal commissions
- Managers: Click "Commission Manager" for team payouts

How Commissions Are Calculated:
- Based on the sold project value
- Uses commission rules set by admin
- Can vary by product type, salesperson, or tier
- Calculated automatically when a deal closes

Step-by-Step Guide:

Viewing Your Commissions (Sales Reps):
1. Click "My Commissions" tab
2. See pending and paid commissions
3. Filter by date range or status
4. Click any record for details

Commission Statuses:
- Pending: Calculated but not yet paid
- Approved: Approved for payout
- Paid: Commission has been paid

Running Payouts (Managers):
1. Go to Commission Manager
2. Review pending commissions
3. Select commissions to pay
4. Click "Process Payout"
5. Mark as paid after issuing checks/deposits

Commission Splits:
If multiple salespeople are on a deal:
- Each rep's percentage is set on the opportunity
- Commissions are automatically split
- Each rep sees their portion in their view

Common Issues:

Commission Not Showing:
- Deal may not be fully closed
- Check if project is in correct status
- Commission rules may not apply to this product

Incorrect Amount:
- Review the commission rule that applied
- Check if discount affected calculation
- Contact admin if discrepancy exists

Tips:
- Check commissions weekly
- Report discrepancies to your manager promptly
- Keep track of commission statements for taxes
- Understand your commission structure
