# Making a Sales Person Inactive in bpmPro

When a sales person leaves the company or changes roles, you need to deactivate them in bpmPro so they no longer appear in sales person dropdown menus. This tutorial walks through the steps to uncheck the **Company Role Active** flag on their contact record.

> **Note:** Making a sales person inactive does **not** delete their record or remove them from existing sales documents. It only removes them from future dropdown selections.

---

## Step 1 — Search for Your Company Account

Use the **global Search box** at the top of bpmPro to search for your company name.

Click on the **Account** result to open your company's account record.

---

## Step 2 — Go to the Contacts Tab

Inside the account record, click on the **Contacts** tab to see all contacts linked to the account.

---

## Step 3 — Find the Sales Person

In the Contacts list, locate the sales person you want to deactivate. You can click on their name to see their details in a popup.

If the contacts list is long, you may need to expand it to see all contacts.

---

## Step 4 — Edit the Contact Record

Open the sales person's contact record. Scroll down to the **Employee Contact Internal Setup** section. You'll see the **Company Role Active** checkbox is currently checked.

Click the **pencil icon** to enter edit mode.

---

## Step 5 — Uncheck Company Role Active

In edit mode, **uncheck** the **Company Role Active** checkbox.
