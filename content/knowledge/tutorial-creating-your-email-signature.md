Creating Your Email Signature

# Creating Your Email Signature in bpmPro

Setting up your email signature ensures that every email sent from bpmPro includes your professional contact information. This quick tutorial walks through the steps to create your signature in your Salesforce profile settings.

---

## Step 1 — Open Your Profile Settings

Click on your **profile icon** at the top-right corner of the screen. This takes you to your profile settings.

---

## Step 2 — Go to Settings

Below your profile picture, click **Settings** to access your personal information settings.

---

## Step 3 — Navigate to My Email Settings

In the Settings menu, use the **Quick Find** toolbar on the left-hand side. Click on **My Email Settings** to open the email settings page.

---

## Step 4 — Enter Your Email Signature

Type your email signature details in the signature text field. Include information like:

- Your full name
- Title / position
- Phone number
- Email address
- Company name and website

---

## Step 5 — Save

Click **Save** to store your email signature. It will now appear automatically on emails sent from bpmPro.

To return to the main application, click **Command Center** or any other menu item.

---

## Quick Reference

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Click your **profile icon** (top-right) |
| 2 | Click **Settings** |
| 3 | Use Quick Find → click **My Email Settings** |
| 4 | Type your signature |
| 5 | Click **Save** |
