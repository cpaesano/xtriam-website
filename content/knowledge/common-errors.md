Common Errors & Troubleshooting

Overview:
This guide covers the most common errors and issues users encounter in bpmPro, along with their solutions.

Login Issues:

"Invalid Username or Password":
- Check for typos in username and password
- Verify caps lock is off
- Try "Forgot Password" if unsure
- Username is usually your email address

"Account Locked":
- Too many failed login attempts
- Contact your admin to unlock
- Or wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock

"Your session has expired":
- Normal after inactivity
- Simply log in again
- Consider enabling "Remember Me"

Quote Errors:

"Missing Required Fields":
- Check for red asterisks on required fields
- Common missing fields: Contact, Close Date, Account

"Cannot Generate PDF":
- All products must have prices
- PDF template must be active
- Try clearing browser cache and retrying

"Missing Product Price":
- Product needs a Price Book entry
- Contact admin to add pricing

Project Errors:

"Cannot Move Project":
- Check your permissions
- Project may be locked
- Contact manager for assistance

"Record Not Found":
- Record may have been deleted
- You may not have access
- Ask the owner to share it

Payment Errors:

"Payment Failed":
- Card was declined by bank
- Ask customer for different card
- Check if card hasn't expired

"Cannot Process Refund":
- Original payment may be too old
- Stripe account may have issues
- Contact admin for assistance

General Troubleshooting Steps:

Step 1: Refresh the Page
- Many issues resolve with a simple refresh
- Use Ctrl+F5 (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) for hard refresh

Step 2: Clear Browser Cache
1. Go to browser settings
2. Clear cache and cookies
3. Log in again

Step 3: Try Different Browser
- Chrome is recommended
- Firefox and Safari also supported
- Internet Explorer is NOT supported

Step 4: Check Internet Connection
- Ensure stable connection
- Try a different network if possible

Step 5: Contact Support
If issue persists, create a support ticket with:
- Error message (screenshot if possible)
- Steps to reproduce
- What you were trying to do

Tips:
- Take screenshots of error messages
- Note what you were doing when the error occurred
- Try the same action on a different record to see if it's specific
- Check if colleagues are experiencing the same issue
