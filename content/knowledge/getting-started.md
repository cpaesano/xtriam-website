Account Setup & Getting Started

Overview:
Welcome to bpmPro! This guide helps new users get set up and familiar with the system.

First-Time Login:

Step 1: Check Your Email
Look for a welcome email from xTriam containing:
- Your username (usually your email address)
- A temporary password or password reset link

Step 2: Log In
1. Go to login.salesforce.com
2. Enter your username
3. Enter your temporary password
4. Click "Log In"

Step 3: Change Your Password
1. You'll be prompted to create a new password
2. Requirements: At least 8 characters, uppercase and lowercase letters, at least one number, at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
3. Save your password securely

Setting Up Your Profile:

Update Profile Information:
1. Click your profile icon (top right corner)
2. Click "Settings" or "Edit Profile"
3. Update: Profile photo, Contact information, Time zone and locale
4. Save changes

Set Up Email Signature:
1. Go to Settings, then Email
2. Click "Email Signature"
3. Create your signature with: Your name, Title, Phone number, Company info
4. Save

Navigating bpmPro:

Main Navigation Elements:
- Tabs: Main navigation at top
- App Launcher: Access all apps (waffle icon)
- Search: Global search bar
- Home: Dashboard and tasks

Key Areas to Explore:
- Accounts: Customer companies
- Contacts: People at those companies
- Opportunities: Potential deals
- Quotes: Proposals with pricing
- Projects: Active jobs

Common Issues:

"Forgot Password":
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your username
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password

"Account Locked":
- Wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock
- Or contact your admin for immediate unlock

"Can't Find Something":
- Use the global search bar
- Check the App Launcher for all apps
- Ask your manager or admin
