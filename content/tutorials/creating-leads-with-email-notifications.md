# Creating Leads with Salesperson Email Notifications in bpmPro

When a new lead comes in, bpmPro can automatically notify the assigned salesperson via email — including the project address, notes, and a direct link to the project record. This tutorial walks through the QuickStart lead creation workflow with email notification enabled.

---

## Step 1 — Start the QuickStart Lead Creation

From the homepage, navigate to the **Leads** section by clicking the menu. This opens the **QuickStart New Lead Creation** workflow.

Enter the lead's contact information:

- **First Name** and **Last Name**
- **Mobile Number**
- **Email Address**
- **Is Lead a Contractor?** — Select No for homeowners, Yes for contractors

---

## Step 2 — Enter the Billing Address

Fill in the address details:

- **Billing Street Address**
- **Billing City**
- **Billing State**
- **Billing ZIP Code**

If the project address is the same as the billing address, leave the default setting. Click **Next** when everything looks correct. The contact information and billing address are now created.

---

## Step 3 — Add Project Details

Fill in the project assignment and classification fields:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Salesperson** | Who will handle this lead |
| **Project Manager** | Who will manage the project |
| **Lead Source** | How the lead came in |
| **Service Type** | Product + Installation, Product Only, or Labor Only |
| **Relationship** | New Client or Repeat Business |
| **Construction Type** | New Residential Construction, Retrofit, etc. |

---

## Step 4 — Add Notes and Enable Notifications

Below the project details, you'll find two important options:

### Special Notes for the Salesperson

Type any notes you want the salesperson to receive in their email notification. The text box can be expanded to accommodate longer messages — for example, special instructions, customer preferences, or urgency notes.

### Send Salesperson Notification Email

This checkbox is **selected by default**. When checked, bpmPro automatically sends an email to the assigned salesperson with the lead details, project address, and any notes you entered.

### Create Sales Document

Optionally, check the **Create Sales Document** checkbox to automatically create a sales document when the project record is created. This saves you from having to create one manually later.

Click **Next** to finish.

---

## Step 5 — View the New Record

After processing, navigate to the **Projects** section. If your list view is set to **Recently Viewed**, the new project will appear at the top. Click on the project record to open the workspace.

---

## Step 6 — Verify the Email Notification

In the project workspace, click the **Activity** tab. You'll see the automatic email in the activity timeline. You can:

- **Expand the entry** to view the full email text
- **Click the header** to open the full notification

### What the Salesperson Receives

The salesperson receives an email on their phone (or desktop) that includes:

- The lead details and notes you entered
- **Project address** — clicking it opens the address in maps
- **Project name link** — clicking it opens the project record directly in bpmPro
- If the salesperson is using the **bpmPro mobile app**, the link opens the app and displays the project details

---

## Quick Reference

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Click **Leads** → enter name, phone, and email |
| 2 | Enter billing address → click **Next** |
| 3 | Select Salesperson, Project Manager, Lead Source, Service Type, Relationship, Construction Type |
| 4 | Add notes → verify **Send Notification Email** is checked → click **Next** |
| 5 | Go to **Projects** → click the new project |
| 6 | Check the **Activity** tab to verify the email was sent |
