# Managing Product and Labor Items in bpmPro

The **Manage Items** functionality is where you build your sales document line by line. This tutorial walks through the complete process — from creating a new sales document to adding product and labor items, cloning items, performing mass updates, and sorting your proposal.

---

## Creating a Sales Document

1. Open an existing project
2. Click the **arrow** and select **New** to create a new sales document
3. Name the document (e.g., the project name + "Residence") — the name is customizable
4. Leave the default settings or adjust as needed
5. Click **Save**

The new sales document is created. Click into it to open the **Sales Document Workspace**, then navigate to the **Sales Document Navigator** and click **Manage Items**.

---

## Adding a New Line Item

Click the **Add New Item** button. Each line item has two sections:

### Product Details (Left Side)

Fill out the product information:

- **Quantity** — Number of units
- **Mark** — Location identifier (e.g., "Living Room", "Master Bedroom")
- **Width** and **Height** — Product dimensions
- **Vendor Quote Number** — Reference number from the vendor
- **Product Series** — The product line
- **Frame Finish** — Color/finish selection
- **Vendor Name** — Select the product vendor
- **Glass Makeup** — Glass specification

### Labor Details (Right Side)

Search for the dealer item by typing in the search bar or browsing your company's **Dealer Price Book**. Once selected, bpmPro automatically populates the preset labor costs.

Additional labor fields include:

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Custom Install Charge** | Override the default installation cost for special requirements |
| **Labor Upcharge** | Additional charge for difficult installations |
| **Flashing** | Flashing labor cost |
| **Stucco** | Stucco repair labor |
| **Drywall** | Drywall repair labor |
| **Ladder** | Ladder equipment charge |
| **Scaffolding** | Scaffolding equipment charge |
| **Installation Type** | New Construction or Retrofit |
| **Size Category** | Standard, Oversized, XL, and more |

### Expanded Fields

Click **Expand** to access additional data fields:

- **Design Pressures** — Wind load ratings
- **U Factor** — Thermal performance
- **Solar Heat Coefficient** — SHGC value
- **Product Approvals** — Certification details
- **Remarks** — Notes for this line item

Click **Save** to store the line item. If an illustration is available for the selected dealer item, it will appear for you to verify the selection.

---

## Cloning Line Items

Instead of creating every item from scratch, use the **Clone** button to duplicate an existing line item. After cloning:

- A summary displays and the cloned item appears as "Clone 1"
- Rename it (e.g., "Dining Room") and adjust the quantity or other details as needed

> **Tip:** Cloning is the fastest way to add similar items. Clone once, then just change the mark, quantity, and dimensions.

---

## Mass Updates

To update multiple line items at once, you can change fields like:

- **Installation Type**
- **Size Category**
- **Vendor**
- **Frame Finish**
- **Glass Makeup**
- **Vendor Quote Number**

Click **Apply** to finalize the changes across all selected items.

---

## Sorting Line Items

You can sort your line items by clicking column headers in the proposal scope of work table:

- Click **Details** to sort by **Mark** (ascending) — click again for descending
- Click the **Line Item** header to sort by **Position/Line Item Number** — click again to reverse

You can also manually assign positions to line items if you need a custom order.

---

## Sold Document Behavior

Once a sales document is marked as **Sold**, you can still view items but cannot add or edit them. To make changes, the document must be reverted to **Open** status first.

---

## Quick Reference

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Add a new item** | Click **Add New Item** → fill product and labor details → **Save** |
| **Clone an item** | Click **Clone** on an existing item → rename and adjust |
| **Mass update** | Change fields in the bulk update bar → click **Apply** |
| **Sort by mark** | Click the **Details** column header |
| **Sort by position** | Click the **Line Item** column header |
| **View expanded fields** | Click **Expand** on any line item |
