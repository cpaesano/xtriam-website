# Making a Sales Person Inactive in bpmPro

When a sales person leaves the company or changes roles, you need to deactivate them in bpmPro so they no longer appear in sales person dropdown menus. This tutorial walks through the steps to uncheck the **Company Role Active** flag on their contact record.

> **Note:** Making a sales person inactive does **not** delete their record or remove them from existing sales documents. It only removes them from future dropdown selections.

---

## Step 1 — Search for Your Company Account

Use the **global Search box** at the top of bpmPro to search for your company name.

![Global search bar highlighted at the top of bpmPro](/images/tutorials/inactive_sp_search_bar.jpeg)

Click on the **Account** result to open your company's account record.

![Search results showing Window Dealer, Inc. account highlighted](/images/tutorials/inactive_sp_search_results.jpeg)

---

## Step 2 — Go to the Contacts Tab

Inside the account record, click on the **Contacts** tab to see all contacts linked to the account.

![Account Workspace with Contacts tab highlighted](/images/tutorials/inactive_sp_contacts_tab.jpeg)

---

## Step 3 — Find the Sales Person

In the Contacts list, locate the sales person you want to deactivate. You can click on their name to see their details in a popup.

![Contacts list with sales person popup showing contact details](/images/tutorials/inactive_sp_contacts_popup.jpeg)

If the contacts list is long, you may need to expand it to see all contacts.

![Full contacts list showing all employee contacts](/images/tutorials/inactive_sp_contacts_list.jpeg)

---

## Step 4 — Edit the Contact Record

Open the sales person's contact record. Scroll down to the **Employee Contact Internal Setup** section. You'll see the **Company Role Active** checkbox is currently checked.

Click the **pencil icon** to enter edit mode.

![Contact record showing Employee Contact Internal Setup with Company Role Active checked and pencil icon highlighted](/images/tutorials/inactive_sp_pencil_edit.jpeg)

---

## Step 5 — Uncheck Company Role Active

In edit mode, **uncheck** the **Company Role Active** checkbox.

![Edit mode showing Company Role Active checkbox unchecked](/images/tutorials/inactive_sp_uncheck_active.jpeg)

> **Important:** This is the key step. Once Company Role Active is unchecked, the sales person will no longer appear in dropdown menus when assigning sales people to quotes, projects, or sales documents.

---

## Step 6 — Save

Click **Save** to apply the change.

![Save button highlighted at the bottom of the form](/images/tutorials/inactive_sp_save.jpeg)

The sales person is now inactive. They will no longer appear in sales person dropdowns throughout bpmPro.

---

## Quick Reference

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Search for your company account |
| 2 | Click the **Contacts** tab |
| 3 | Find the sales person in the contacts list |
| 4 | Open their contact record → click the **pencil icon** |
| 5 | Uncheck **Company Role Active** |
| 6 | Click **Save** |
