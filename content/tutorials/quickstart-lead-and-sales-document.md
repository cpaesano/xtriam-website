# QuickStart: Create a Lead and Sales Document in bpmPro

The QuickStart flow is the fastest way to enter a new lead in bpmPro. It creates the account, contact, project, and (optionally) a sales document all at once. This tutorial walks through the complete process step by step.

> **Important:** Before creating a new lead, always search first to make sure the contact doesn't already exist in the system. This prevents duplicate records.

---

## Step 1 — Search for the Contact First

Click on the **Search** bar at the top of the screen and type the contact's name to check if they already exist.

![Global search bar highlighted at the top of bpmPro](/images/tutorials/quickstart_search_first.png)

If the search returns no matching results, you know it's safe to create a new lead.

![Search results for Michael Jackson showing no existing matches](/images/tutorials/quickstart_search_no_results.png)

---

## Step 2 — Open the Leads Tab and Start the QuickStart Flow

Click on the **Leads** tab in the top navigation. The **QuickStart New Lead Creation** form will appear on the left side of the screen.

Fill out the contact information:

- **First Name** and **Last Name** (required)
- **Mobile Number** (required)
- **Email Address** (required)
- **Is Lead a Contractor?** — Select No for homeowners, Yes for contractor leads

![QuickStart New Lead Creation form showing contact fields and Is Lead a Contractor dropdown](/images/tutorials/quickstart_lead_form_empty.png)

---

## Step 3 — Enter the Billing and Project Address

Fill in the address details:

- **Billing Street Address** (required)
- **Billing City** (required)
- **Billing State** (required)
- **Billing Zip Code**
- **Is Project Address the same as Billing Address?** — Select Yes if they match

![Address fields with street address filled in](/images/tutorials/quickstart_address_fields.png)

Click **Next** to continue to the project details screen.

---

## Step 4 — Enter Project Details

On the second screen, bpmPro confirms the contact information was saved and presents the project detail fields.

Fill in the following:

- **Sales Person** — Select the assigned sales person
- **Project Manager** — Select the project manager (required)
- **Lead Source** — How the lead came in (e.g., Purchased List, Referral, Web)
- **Service Type** — Product + Installation, Product Only, or Labor Only

![Project details form showing Sales Person, Project Manager, Lead Source, and Service Type fields](/images/tutorials/quickstart_project_details.png)

Continue filling in:

- **Relationship** — New Client or Existing Client
- **Construction Type** — Retrofit - Residential, New Construction, etc.

![Project details with all fields filled including Service Type and Construction Type](/images/tutorials/quickstart_project_filled.png)

---

## Step 5 — Check Create Sales Document

At the bottom of the form, check the **Create Sales Document** checkbox if you want bpmPro to automatically create a sales document for this lead.

![Create Sales Document checkbox highlighted at the bottom of the project details form](/images/tutorials/quickstart_create_salesdoc_checkbox.png)

> **Tip:** Always check this box if you plan to start quoting right away. It saves you from having to create the sales document manually later.

Click **Next** to finish.

---

## Step 6 — Access the Created Records

After the flow completes, bpmPro will have created the account, contact, project, and sales document. Click the **Sales Documents** icon to go directly to the newly created sales document.

![Sales Documents icon shown after flow completion](/images/tutorials/quickstart_salesdocs_icon.png)

You'll see the new sales document at the top of the **Recently Viewed** list.

![Sales Documents list showing the newly created Michael Jackson Proposal at the top](/images/tutorials/quickstart_salesdocs_list.png)

---

## Quick Reference

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | **Search first** — Check that the contact doesn't already exist |
| 2 | Click **Leads** tab → Fill out name, phone, and email |
| 3 | Enter billing/project address → Click **Next** |
| 4 | Select Sales Person, Project Manager, Lead Source, Service Type, Relationship, Construction Type |
| 5 | Check **Create Sales Document** → Click **Next** |
| 6 | Click the **Sales Documents** icon to access the new record |
