# Entering New Leads in bpmPro

There are two ways to enter a new lead in bpmPro. Choose the approach that fits your workflow.

---

## Approach #1 — Manual (3-Step Process)

This approach gives you full control over each record. Follow these three steps in order:

1. **Create the Account** — See the [Creating Accounts](/support/tutorials/creating-accounts) tutorial
2. **Create the Contact** — See the [Creating Contacts](/support/tutorials/creating-contacts) tutorial
3. **Create the Project** — From the account record, click **New Project** to create a project linked to the account and contact

After the project is created, you can create a Sales Document from within the project.

> **When to use this approach:** When you need to set up detailed account information, multiple contacts, or when the account already exists in the system.

---

## Approach #2 — QuickStart New Lead Creation

The QuickStart flow lets you create an account, contact, project, and (optionally) a sales document all in one step. This is the fastest way to enter a new lead.

### Step 1 — Open the Leads Tab

Click on the **Leads** tab in the top navigation bar. The QuickStart New Lead Creation flow will appear on the left side of the screen.

Fill out the contact and address information:

- **First Name** and **Last Name** (required)
- **Mobile Number** and **Email Address** (required)
- **Billing and Project Address** — Street, city, state, and zip code
- **Is Project Address the same as Billing Address?** — Select Yes if they match

![QuickStart New Lead Creation flow showing contact information and address fields](/images/tutorials/leads_quickstart_flow.jpeg)

Click **Next** to continue.

---

### Step 2 — Enter Project Details and Create a Sales Document

On the second screen, you can optionally enter project details:

- **Sales Person** and **Project Manager**
- **Lead Source** — How the lead came in (e.g., Company Truck, Referral, Web)
- **Service Type** — Product + Installation, Product Only, or Labor Only
- **Relationship** — New Client or Existing Client
- **Construction Type** — Retrofit Residential, New Construction, etc.

**Important:** Check the **Create Sales Document** checkbox if you want bpmPro to automatically create a sales document for this lead. Enter a name for the document (e.g., "Robert Lewis Proposal").

![QuickStart flow second screen with project details and Create Sales Document checkbox highlighted](/images/tutorials/leads_create_salesdoc_checkbox.jpeg)

Click **Next** to finish.

---

### Step 3 — Access the Created Records

After the flow completes, bpmPro will have created the account, contact, project, and (if selected) the sales document. You can access the newly created records by clicking the **Projects** or **Sales Documents** icons shown at the bottom of the screen.

![Completed flow showing Projects and Sales Documents navigation icons](/images/tutorials/leads_after_flow_completion.jpeg)

---

## Quick Reference

| Approach | Steps | Best For |
|----------|-------|----------|
| **Manual** | Create Account → Contact → Project → Sales Document | Detailed setup, existing accounts |
| **QuickStart** | Leads tab → Fill out flow → Everything created at once | Fast entry, new leads |
