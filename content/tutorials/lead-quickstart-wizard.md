# Lead Quick Start Wizard

The Lead Quick Start Wizard is the fastest way to capture a new lead from an incoming call. It creates the Project, Account, Contact, and Sales Document automatically, assigns the team, schedules a sales appointment, and sends notifications to both the rep and the client — all in one guided flow.

> **What this wizard does:**
> - Captures the client's contact and project information in one guided flow
> - Assigns a sales rep and project manager and shows their live calendar availability
> - Creates the Project, Account, Contact, and Sales Document automatically
> - Schedules the sales appointment and sends notifications to both the rep and the client

---

## Step 1 — Enter Contact Information

Go to **Leads** in the top navigation — the Quick Start: New Lead wizard opens automatically.

Enter the client's **First Name**, **Last Name**, **Mobile Phone**, and **Email**. The Team Calendar on the right shows current sales rep availability, and the Lead Pipeline below shows the unquoted count per rep.

![Quick Start New Lead wizard showing contact information fields with Team Calendar and Lead Pipeline sidebar](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step1_contact_info.png)

Click **Next** to continue.

---

## Step 2 — Enter the Address

Enter the client's Billing Address: **Street**, **City**, **State**, and **Zip**.

Check **Same as billing address** if the project is at the same location — the Project Address will auto-fill.

![Address step showing billing address fields and Same as billing address checkbox](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step2_address.png)

Click **Next** to continue.

---

## Step 3 — Enter Project Information & Assign the Team

Select the **Service Type**, **Lead Source**, **Construction Type**, and **Relationship** (New or Repeat Client).

Assign the **Sales Person** and **Project Manager** from the dropdowns.

Enter any **Notes to Sales Person** — these will appear in the notification email sent to the rep.

![Project information step showing service type, lead source, team assignment, and notes fields](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step3_project_info.png)

Click **Review** to proceed to the summary.

---

## Step 4 — Team Calendar View

The Team Calendar updates in real time as you work through the wizard. The assigned sales rep is highlighted with an **ASSIGNED** badge.

Use this to confirm availability before setting the appointment.

![Team Calendar showing day view with assigned sales rep highlighted and existing appointments](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step4_team_calendar.png)

---

## Step 5 — Review Your Lead

Review all Contact and Project details before creating the record. Confirm the Sales Person and Project Manager assignments.

Two options are pre-checked:

- **Create Sales Document** — automatically creates a Sales Doc linked to the project
- **Send Notification to Sales Person** — emails the assigned rep immediately

![Review step showing contact and project summary with Create Sales Document and Send Notification checkboxes](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step5_review.png)

Click **Create Lead** to finalize.

---

## Step 6 — Lead Created — Schedule the Appointment

A green confirmation banner confirms the lead, project, and Sales Doc were created.

The wizard immediately offers to schedule a Sales Appointment. Select the **Employee**, **Date**, **Start/End Time** — the subject auto-fills with the client's name.

![Lead created confirmation with schedule appointment form and Team Calendar sidebar](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step6_schedule.png)

Click **Schedule & View Project**, or skip if no appointment is needed yet.

---

## Step 7 — Appointment Scheduled Confirmation

A modal confirms the appointment was added to the calendar. bpmPro offers to send a confirmation email with a calendar invite to the client.

![Appointment Scheduled modal offering to send confirmation email to the client](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step7_appointment_scheduled.png)

Click **Send Confirmation** to notify the client, or **Skip** to move on.

---

## Step 8 — Confirmation Sent to Client

A second modal confirms the email was sent successfully. The client receives the appointment details and a calendar invite at their email address.

![Confirmation Sent modal showing the client will receive appointment details and calendar invite](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step8_confirmation_sent.png)

Click **Done - View Project** to go directly to the project record.

---

## Step 9 — Activity Log on the Project

The Project record's Activity tab automatically logs everything that was created. The New Lead Assigned email sent to the sales rep is recorded as a sent email activity, and the upcoming Sales Appointment appears under **Upcoming & Overdue**.

![Project Activity tab showing the New Lead Assigned email and upcoming Sales Appointment](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step9_activity_log.png)

---

## Step 10 — Sales Rep Receives Lead Notification Email

The assigned sales rep receives a **New Lead Assigned** email immediately. The email includes all client information, project details, lead source, and the notes entered during the wizard. A **View Project** button links directly to the project record in bpmPro.

![New Lead Assigned email showing client information, project details, and notes](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step10_rep_email.png)

---

## Step 11 — Client Receives Appointment Confirmation Email

The client receives a branded **Appointment Confirmation** email. It includes the date, time, location, and the sales rep's name and contact. An `appointment.ics` file is attached so the client can add it to their phone or computer calendar.

![Appointment Confirmation email with date, time, location, representative info, and calendar invite attachment](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step11_client_email.png)

---

## Step 12 — Appointment Syncs to Google Calendar

The appointment appears on the sales rep's Google Calendar automatically. No manual calendar entry is needed — everything syncs from bpmPro.

![Google Calendar showing the sales appointment synced from bpmPro](/images/tutorials/lead_quickstart_step12_google_calendar.png)
