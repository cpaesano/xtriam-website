# Updating Product Installation Items in bpmPro

bpmPro stores all your dealer item pricing and material settings in the **Dealer Items** section of the Command Center. These settings control installation costs for new construction and retrofit jobs, additional work and equipment charges, screw selections, woodbuck specifications, caulking, and flashing — all organized into six list views that you can edit inline.

---

## Step 1 — Navigate to Dealer Items

Go to the **Command Center** and scroll down to the **Labor Pricebook Setup** section. Click **Dealer Items** to open the dealer items list.

![Command Center showing Labor Pricebook Setup section with Dealer Items, Dealer Item Manager Page, Add-On Products, and Building Materials](/images/tutorials/update_prod_command_center.jpeg)

---

## Step 2 — Select a List View

The Dealer Items page opens to your pinned (default) list view. Click the **dropdown arrow** next to the list view name to switch between views.

![List view dropdown showing all six views: 1 ~ Product Installation Items, 2 ~ Additional Work and Equipment, 3 ~ Screws for Items, 4 ~ Woodbucks for Items, 5 ~ Caulking for Items, 6 ~ Flashing for Items](/images/tutorials/update_prod_list_views.jpeg)

bpmPro provides six pre-built list views:

| List View | What It Controls |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **1 ~ Product Installation Items** | New construction and retrofit installation costs per product |
| **2 ~ Additional Work and Equipment** | Drywall, stucco, ladder, and scaffolding charges |
| **3 ~ Screws for Items** | Product and woodbuck screw selections and spacing |
| **4 ~ Woodbucks for Items** | Woodbuck material selection and pricing |
| **5 ~ Caulking for Items** | Caulking product selection per dealer item |
| **6 ~ Flashing for Items** | Flashing specifications per dealer item |

---

## Editing Product Installation Items (View 1)

Select **1 ~ Product Installation Items** from the dropdown. This view shows every dealer item with columns for **New Construction** and **Retrofit Installation** costs at multiple size tiers.

![Product Installation Items list view showing dealer items with New Construction and Retrofit Installation cost columns](/images/tutorials/update_prod_installation_list.jpeg)

### Inline Editing

Click the **pencil icon** next to any cost field to make it editable. Type the new value directly in the cell.

![Inline editing showing a cost field being edited with value 350](/images/tutorials/update_prod_inline_edit.jpeg)

You can edit **multiple entries** before saving. Each edited field turns yellow to indicate unsaved changes.

![Multiple fields edited showing yellow highlighted cells with values 200 and 400](/images/tutorials/update_prod_multiple_edits.jpeg)

When you're done, click **Save** at the bottom of the list to save all changes at once, or **Cancel** to discard them.

![Save and Cancel buttons at the bottom of the list with multiple edited entries](/images/tutorials/update_prod_save_cancel.jpeg)

> **Tip:** You can edit as many rows as you need before clicking Save. bpmPro saves all changes in a single batch, which is much faster than editing one item at a time.

---

## Editing Additional Work and Equipment (View 2)

Select **2 ~ Additional Work and Equipment** to update charges for drywall labor, stucco labor, ladder labor, ladder equipment, scaffolding equipment, and scaffolding labor.

![Additional Work and Equipment list view showing drywall, stucco, ladder, and scaffolding columns](/images/tutorials/update_prod_additional_work.jpeg)

The same inline editing process applies — click the pencil icon, update the values, and click Save.

---

## Editing Screws for Items (View 3)

Select **3 ~ Screws for Items** to manage screw specifications for each dealer item. This view shows:

- **Product Screw Spacing** — Number of screws per item
- **Product Screw Selection** — The screw type (e.g., "Ultracon Screw 1/4 x 2-3/4 Phillips Flat Head")
- **Wood Buck Screw Selection** — Screw type for woodbucks

![Screws for Items list view showing screw selections and spacing for each dealer item](/images/tutorials/update_prod_screws_list.jpeg)

To change a screw type, click the field and search for or select from the available building materials.

![Product Screw Selection field showing inline search for screw type](/images/tutorials/update_prod_screw_selection.jpeg)

---

## Editing Woodbucks for Items (View 4)

Select **4 ~ Woodbucks for Items** to update woodbuck material selections and pricing per dealer item.

![Woodbucks for Items list view showing woodbuck screw counts, wood buck selection, and pricing columns](/images/tutorials/update_prod_woodbucks_list.jpeg)

---

## Editing Caulking and Flashing (Views 5 & 6)

Select **5 ~ Caulking for Items** to update caulking product selections, or **6 ~ Flashing for Items** for flashing specifications.

![Caulking for Items list view showing caulking selection column with product descriptions](/images/tutorials/update_prod_caulking_list.jpeg)

---

## Quick Reference

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Go to **Command Center** → **Labor Pricebook Setup** → **Dealer Items** |
| 2 | Click the list view dropdown to select the category you want to update |
| 3 | Click the **pencil icon** on any field to edit it inline |
| 4 | Update as many fields as needed (edited cells turn yellow) |
| 5 | Click **Save** to save all changes, or **Cancel** to discard |
