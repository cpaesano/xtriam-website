# Adding a New Sales Person in bpmPro

To add a new sales person in bpmPro, you create them as a **contact** under your company's account and assign them the **Sales and Estimating** internal company role. This ensures they appear in sales person dropdowns throughout the system — such as when creating quotes, proposals, and projects.

> **Note:** This same process applies to adding any employee (project managers, operations staff, etc.) — the only difference is the Internal Company Role you select.

---

## Step 1 — Find Your Company Account

Use the **global Search box** at the top of bpmPro to search for your company name. Click on the **Account** result to open your company's account record.

![Global search results showing Window Dealer, Inc. account highlighted](/images/tutorials/salesperson_search_company.png)

---

## Step 2 — Go to the Contacts Tab

Inside the account record, click on the **Contacts** tab to see all contacts currently linked to the account.

![Account Workspace with Contacts tab highlighted](/images/tutorials/salesperson_contacts_tab.png)

---

## Step 3 — Click New

In the Contacts section, click the **New** button to create a new contact.

![Contacts list with New button highlighted](/images/tutorials/salesperson_new_button.png)

---

## Step 4 — Fill Out Contact Information

Fill out the new contact form with the sales person's details:

- **First Name** and **Last Name** (required)
- **Title** — Enter the employee's title (e.g., "Sales Rep")

![New Contact form with name fields and Title set to Sales Rep](/images/tutorials/salesperson_contact_form.png)

---

## Step 5 — Set Category to Employee

Click the **Category** dropdown and select **Employee**. This is important — it identifies this contact as a team member rather than a customer.

![Category dropdown showing Employee option highlighted](/images/tutorials/salesperson_category_employee.png)

---

## Step 6 — Enter Mobile and Email

Fill in the sales person's **Mobile** number and **Email** address. These are used for communication and notifications within bpmPro.

![Mobile and Email fields filled in with Employee Contact Internal Setup section visible below](/images/tutorials/salesperson_mobile_email.png)

---

## Step 7 — Set Internal Company Role

Scroll down to the **Employee Contact Internal Setup** section. Click the **Internal Company Role** dropdown and select **Sales and Estimating**.

![Internal Company Role dropdown showing all roles with Sales and Estimating highlighted](/images/tutorials/salesperson_role_sales_estimating.png)

---

## Step 8 — Check Company Role Active

Check the **Company Role Active** checkbox. This is critical — without it, the sales person will **not appear** in dropdown menus throughout bpmPro.

![Company Role Active checkbox checked](/images/tutorials/salesperson_role_active.png)

> **Why this matters:** If you skip this step, the new sales person won't show up when you try to assign them to quotes, projects, or sales documents. Always check this box for any active employee.

---

## Step 9 — Save

Click **Save** to create the contact.

![Save button highlighted at the bottom of the contact form](/images/tutorials/salesperson_save.png)

The new sales person will now appear in all sales person dropdown menus across bpmPro.

---

## Quick Reference

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Search for your company account |
| 2 | Click the **Contacts** tab |
| 3 | Click **New** |
| 4 | Enter **First Name**, **Last Name**, and **Title** (e.g., "Sales Rep") |
| 5 | Set **Category** to **Employee** |
| 6 | Enter **Mobile** and **Email** |
| 7 | Set **Internal Company Role** to **Sales and Estimating** |
| 8 | Check **Company Role Active** |
| 9 | Click **Save** |
