Frequently Asked Questions

What does Stripe charge per transaction?

Stripe's standard processing fee is 2.9% + $0.30 per successful card charge. There are no monthly fees, setup fees, or hidden costs. You only pay when you get paid.

Do I need a separate Stripe account?

Yes, you'll need your own Stripe account, which is free to create. During bpmPro setup, our team helps you connect it to your CRM so payments flow seamlessly into your project records.

Can my customers pay with ACH or bank transfer?

Yes. Stripe supports ACH bank transfers in addition to all major credit and debit cards. ACH transfers have lower processing fees (0.8%, capped at $5), which is ideal for larger project payments.

How do payment reminders work?

bpmPro tracks outstanding balances on every project. You can send automated payment reminder emails with a fresh payment link, so customers can pay with one click. No more awkward phone calls chasing payments.

Is this available on all bpmPro plans?

Stripe payment processing is available on both the Professional and Enterprise plans. There's no extra charge from bpmPro for using the payment feature — you only pay Stripe's standard transaction fees.

Can I track payments alongside my QuickBooks data?

Yes. bpmPro integrates with QuickBooks, so payment data flows into your accounting system. Deposits, progress payments, and final payments are all tracked in both bpmPro and QuickBooks for clean, accurate books.
