If you use ES Windows for quoting, bpmPro lets you import those quotes directly into Salesforce — eliminating double data entry and reducing errors. Your ES Windows pricing feeds straight into bpmPro proposals, keeping everything in one system. Learn more about the ES Windows integration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using Salesforce for your window contracting business.

Is bpmPro built on Salesforce?

Yes. bpmPro is a Salesforce 2GP (second-generation) managed package. It runs natively on the Salesforce platform, which means you get all the benefits of Salesforce — security, uptime, reporting, mobile access, and extensibility — with features purpose-built for window and door contractors. It is not a separate application that connects to Salesforce; it IS Salesforce, customized for your industry.

Do I need a separate Salesforce license?

No. bpmPro includes Salesforce licensing as part of your subscription. You do not need to purchase a separate Salesforce license or navigate Salesforce's licensing model on your own. Everything is bundled into one straightforward subscription so you can get started quickly without worrying about licensing complexity.

Can I customize bpmPro?

Absolutely. Since bpmPro runs on Salesforce, you have full access to the Salesforce platform's customization tools. Create custom fields to track data unique to your business. Build Salesforce Flows to automate repetitive tasks. Design reports and dashboards that surface the metrics you care about. Add validation rules to enforce data quality. And with over 5,000 AppExchange apps available, you can extend bpmPro's capabilities even further — all without writing code.

How is bpmPro different from other window contractor CRMs?

Unlike competitors like Builder Prime, JobNimbus, or LeadPerfection, bpmPro runs natively on Salesforce — the world's #1 CRM platform. This gives you enterprise-grade security (SOC 2 certified), 99.9% guaranteed uptime, and access to 5,000+ AppExchange integrations that proprietary platforms simply cannot match. Additionally, bpmPro was built by a former window installer who has been in the industry since 2003, so every feature is designed around real window and door contractor workflows — not generic home improvement processes.
